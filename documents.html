FAQ

Documents

What documents are needed for clearing customs?

Typically, you need the bill of lading, invoice, export certificate, and any required inspection certificates.

How long does it take to receive the shipping documents?

Shipping documents are sent within 3-5 business days after the vessel's departure.

What is an export certificate?

An export certificate is an official document that certifies the vehicle is allowed to leave the country of origin.

What is a bill of lading?

A bill of lading is a document issued by a carrier to acknowledge receipt of cargo for shipment.

Who receives the original export certificate?

The original export certificate is sent to the buyer or their nominated agent for customs clearance.

What should I do if I lose the documents?

Contact us immediately if you lose any documents. We will assist you in obtaining copies or replacements.
