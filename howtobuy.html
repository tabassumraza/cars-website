How do I import used cars from UK?

The easiest way to import used cars and vehicles from UK takes only a few steps

Choose a Car

  1. Explore our Stock. We have  over 3,000 vehicles for you to choose from.
  2. Select your car, and click send inquiry. Or just send an inquiry directly if you're looking for a specific car.
  3. A sales consultant will contact you within 24 hours.
  4. After agreeing on the price, we will issue you a proforma invoice.
  5. Process your payment within three business days.  See our bank details here.
     Once payment is confirmed, your car will be prepared for shipping.

What if I can’t find the vehicle I want in your stock?

If you can’t find the vehicle you want in our stock, please send us an inquiry at info@autowingsint.com We have access to more than 80,000 used cars sold in British auctions every week. Let us know the vehicle you are looking for, what your budget is and when you intend to receive it.

Information Request

To complete all documents and shipment procedures, we will require some information from you :

  • The consignee name
  • The notify party
  • Your DHL address (where all the necessary documents will be sent)

Shipping Starts

    1. Book the fastest available route.

After the shipment arrives at the port, clear the vehicle with customs and your get the vehicle.
